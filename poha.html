Poha Chivda Recipe

Description

Crunchy and moreish poha chivda is a snack favourite from Maharashtra, savoury with a hint of sweetness, that is enjoyed around the nation. It is especially popular during Diwali when much sweet and savoury making commences at home for festive snacking and visiting guests.

Ingradients

  • 200 mixed nuts
  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • Pinch of hing
  • 15 curry leaves
  • 250gm thin poha
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. In a wok or kadai,dry roast the nuts and raisins for a minute on medium heat.
  2. tip them into a bowl and warm the ghee.
  3. mix through the nuts and raisins.then add salt to taste and take off the heat.
  4. Leave to cool.
